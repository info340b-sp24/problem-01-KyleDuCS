Tony Stark

This character originates from the MCU (Marvel Cinematic Universe) and is portrayed by Robert Downey Jr. Tony Stark is a billionaire, genius and arms developer of Stark Industries. He evenetually developed the Iron Man suit and would use to defeat Earth's greatest enemies. He would found the avengers and become the leader of the team. He would be eventually be defeated by Thanos as he had to use the infinity stones to defeat Thanos's army. To learn more about Tony Stark's story and life check out this wiki page.
